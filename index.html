About Us

At SnakesFit Training, whether you're looking to improve your fitness, learn how to protect yourself, or give your child the tools to handle bullying with confidence and resilience, you're in the right place.

My mission is simple: to empower individuals through physical fitness, self-defence, and personal growth. My classes are designed not just to teach practical skills, but to build lasting confidence, discipline, and respect, both for yourself and others.

No matter your age, gender, or fitness level, you'll find a welcoming environment here. Every session is focused on real-world benefits:

  • Improved physical health and endurance
  • Practical self-defence techniques for everyday safety
  • Mental resilience and confidence building
  • A supportive community that values respect and personal growth
  • Bullying prevention strategies for kids and teens

Whether you're a parent looking for a positive outlet for your child, a woman wanting to feel safer and stronger, or someone just starting their fitness journey, we tailor every program to fit your goals.

Come train with me and see how much you're truly capable of.
